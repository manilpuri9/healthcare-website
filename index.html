TRANSFORMING HEALTHCARE IN INDIA

Transforming healthcare in India by developing a high-tech, energy efficient, eco-friendly hospitalsystem as a national model that can be expanded across the country



Features




Develop a XXXX bed quaternary care, multi-super-specialty hospital with XXXX secondary care satellite clinics in India to establish a model for country-wide expansion

Undertake a new healthcare venture to establish a model for rapid expansion to take advantage of the projected growth in the healthcare sector

The hospital and satellite clinics will be managed and run by reputed clinicians, including super-sub-specialists and specialty nurses, supported by cutting-edge technology

The hospital will be energy efficient using solar power, and will use Card Technologies Nanocar+ to purify water and remove bacteria and heavy metals, establishing a model and technology that can be independently provided across the country

The Hospital will be endowed with specialties for Cardiac Sciences, Gynecology, Obstetrics, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, Oncology, Renal Sciences, Gastroenterology and Neurology, and have state-of-art surgical and medical facilities including a complete mother and child center with adherence to international standard
